A little about NF:

NF is an American rapper, singer and songwriter and he released his first album when he was only 18 years old.

NFs most successful song is called Let you Down from his Perception album.

He had a very hard childhood and grew up in a broken home, which has shaped who he is and what he raps about.

NF raps about his life experiences and the struggles he has dealt with and still deals with.

His mother overdosed on pills in 2009, and he talks about the struggles he faced with his mom in many of his songs.

If you're interested check out Nf's youtube: Youtube.com/NFrealmusic

Albums

  • Moments (2010)
  • Mansion (2014)
  • Therapy Session (2016)
  • Perception (2017)
  • The Search (2019)

NF also recently released a mixtape called Clouds in March of 2021.
